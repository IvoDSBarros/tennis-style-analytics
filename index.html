Tennis Playing Styles

New Generation vs Big 3

Using Principal Component Analysis (PCA) of 21 elite tennis players spanning 2010-2024, this interactive visualization compares playing styles across Clay, Grass, and Hard courts. How do the new stars compare against the Big 3? Are they following their blueprint or forging their own path? A CrewAI-powered senior tennis analyst delivers the insights for you.

About the method

Credit to Jeff Sackmann's Match Charting Project, whose outstanding shot-level data makes this PCA possible. Because the data is sparse (especially on grass), the analysis is focused on 2010-2024, kept only players with ≥5 matches per surface (21 players), and sampled 8 matches per player. PCA variable selection was then optimized per surface. For Clay and Grass, statistical rules selected 4 variables while for Hard an exhaustive search across 12,441 combinations found a superior 4-variable set. This mixed approach provides the best of both statistical rigor and explanatory power. Click below to deep dive into the methodology.
